**THE COMPLETE PROMPT**

Build a complete, production grade website for Fastident Dental Clinic, a real dental practice located at Suite 4, All Seasons Plaza, Federal Junction, 1st Avenue, Gowon Estate, Ipaja Ayobo Road, Ipaja, Lagos, Nigeria. Open Monday to Saturday. Bookings line 09063768685, alternate line 09063898915. Instagram and Facebook handle is fastident.

**Visual direction**

Clinical trust mixed with warmth, not cold or sterile. Soft whites, a calming primary color, deep teal or medical blue, pick one and stay consistent throughout, a clean accent color like soft mint or coral reserved for call to action buttons only. Generous whitespace, rounded corners on cards and buttons, no clip art icons, no generic stock photo smiles unless real clinic photos are unavailable then use tasteful abstract dental iconography instead. Clean sans serif typography, one font with slightly more character for headings, one neutral workhorse font for body text. No purple gradients, no overused SaaS template look. This needs to feel like a real premium Lagos healthcare brand.

**Splash and loading**

A branded loading screen with the clinic logo or wordmark animating in, a subtle tooth or smile icon, should resolve in under one second, no spinner that drags or feels heavy.

**Hero section**

Rotating background slideshow instead of one static image, three to five real clinic photos crossfading every five to six seconds, exterior shot, reception, a treatment room, the team, a close up of equipment. Subtle dark gradient overlay so headline text stays readable no matter which image is showing. Small dot indicators at the bottom so users know multiple images exist, rotation pauses on hover so it doesn't distract someone trying to read. All hero images preload so transitions never stutter, with a solid color fallback if an image fails. Strong headline about confident smiles, primary CTA to book an appointment, secondary CTA to call now.

**Home page**

After the hero, a trust badges row showing years active, patients served, Mon to Sat hours, services overview grid linking into the full services page, a why choose us section addressing dental anxiety and affordability directly, a real patient testimonials carousel pulled from their actual reviews about professionalism and patient comfort, a before and after smile gallery section, a map embed showing the exact Ipaja location, a final CTA banner before the footer.

**Services page**

Full cards for general dental care, tooth extractions, professional teeth cleaning, teeth whitening, missing tooth and teeth replacement, tooth fillings, teeth alignment and orthodontic treatment, management of mouth odour, dental implants, incision and drainage, scaling and polishing, fluoride therapy, root canal treatment, bridges, porcelain fused to metal crowns, removable partial dentures. Each service has a short description, what to expect, and a book this service button that pre fills the booking form. Add a price transparency element, even a starting from range per service, this builds trust and cuts down time wasting inquiries.

**Booking system, the core of the site**

Multi step flow. Step one, select service. Step two, select date and time from real available slots, with a live availability indicator showing today's slots filling up, pulled from actual calendar data, not faked. Step three, patient details, full name, phone number, email, first visit or returning patient, specific concern or note. Step four, confirmation screen with summary and reference number. Booking sends a notification to clinic staff via email or WhatsApp, and a confirmation to the patient. Include a clean printable version of the confirmation the patient can show at reception. Calendar syncs to a real admin view. Reschedule and cancel available via a link in the confirmation message. Automated reminder sent 24 hours before the appointment via SMS or WhatsApp to cut down no shows. Full field validation everywhere, correct Nigerian phone format, correct email format, no booking dates in the past, no empty submissions. A privacy consent checkbox on the form since health adjacent data is being collected, linking to a real privacy policy page.

Separate from this, an emergency contact pathway, clearly marked, for severe pain or trauma cases, skipping the multi step form entirely and going straight to a priority call or WhatsApp number, because people in pain will not fill out four steps.

A patient portal lite, where returning patients can look up booking history or an upcoming appointment using just their phone number, no full account system required.

**Admin dashboard**

Simple, login protected, not public facing. Staff can view upcoming bookings, mark them confirmed or completed, block off unavailable time slots, see basic patient contact info.

**About page**

Clinic story framed around being a trusted neighborhood practice in Ipaja, meet the team section with photos and credentials once provided, clinic environment photos, an equipment and hygiene standards section since cleanliness is a known strength in their existing reviews.

**Gallery page**

Clinic interior, equipment, smile transformations, with filtering by treatment type, whitening, implants, alignment, so prospective patients can find relevant proof fast.

**Testimonials and reviews page**

Full reviews structured as cards with name, star rating, and quote, pulled from their real patient feedback, plus a leave a review button linking out to Google.

**Contact page**

Full address with embedded map, both phone numbers click to call on mobile, a WhatsApp click to chat button, an opening hours table Monday through Saturday, a general contact form separate from the booking form for non appointment questions, social links.

**FAQ page**

Cost ranges if provided, insurance and HMO acceptance, what to expect on a first visit, pain management, appointment policy, walk ins versus booking ahead.

**Trust and conversion extras**

Insurance and HMO logo row if the clinic accepts any plans. A referral or family package mention if applicable, common in Nigerian healthcare marketing and worth highlighting if it exists.

**Global elements on every page**

Sticky header with logo, navigation, a persistent book appointment button always visible. A floating WhatsApp chat button in the bottom corner. Footer with address, both phone numbers, hours, social icons, quick links, and a small map thumbnail. Mobile first responsive design since most bookings will come from phones. Fast load times, optimized images, accessible color contrast, every form keyboard navigable, alt text on every image.

**Technical and SEO requirements**

Clean semantic HTML, no broken links, no leftover placeholder text anywhere in the final build, every button functional, every form actually submits and stores or sends data somewhere real. Cross browser tested, zero console errors, no layout shift on load. Smooth scroll and light scroll reveal animations, nothing flashy or distracting. Proper meta titles and descriptions per page, structured data markup for a local dental business so it displays correctly in Google search and maps, sitemap, favicon, social share preview image. Analytics wired in from day one so the clinic can see where visitors come from and where people drop off in the booking flow. A custom branded 404 page instead of a default browser error, plus an offline fallback message if connection drops mid booking so users don't think their data vanished. Every interactive element needs an empty, loading, success, and error state designed, not just the happy path.

Go through the entire site as a nervous first time patient on a phone with a slow connection, then as the clinic owner verifying every detail is accurate, then as Google indexing it for local search. Fix anything that fails any of those three checks.
